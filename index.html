Hello!

I am a newly graduate master student at KU Leuven. My master thesis is mixrandregret: A command for fitting mixed random regret minimization models using Stata [Github], supervised by Professor Martina Vandebroek; under the guidance of Álvaro A. Gutiérrez Vargas.

I completed a MSc in Economics at Barcelona SE. My economic master thesis is Evolution of Cooperation in Multi-level Public Goods Game with Mobility [PDF], supervised by Professor Larbi Alaoui. I did my undergraduate at Wuhan University, where I got a BSc in Economics. During my bachelor years, I spent a semester exchange at University College Dublin.

My research interests lie in Decision Sciences, Applied Economics, and Applied Econometrics: Regret Theory, Prosocial Behavior, and Machine Learning and AI in Decision Sciences; Labor Economics, Political Economics, Development Economics, Family Economics, and Health Economics; Applied Machine Learning implemented in Econometrics and Health Econometrics. More specifically, I am interested in the topics of decision making, human capital accumulation, inequality, discrimination, unemployment, social safety net, and social networks.
