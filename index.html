Hello!

I am a second year master student at KU Leuven, currently working on mata coding mixed random regret minimization model for my master thesis（supervised by Professor Martina Vandebroek; under the guidance of Álvaro A. Gutiérrez Vargas). I am also interested in policy impacts on employees and households, and I am a part-time research assistant on family economics for Professor Guo Rufei. My research interest lies in labor economics, development economics, micro theory and econometrics.

I did my undergraduate at Wuhan University, where I got BSc in Economics. During my bachelor years, I spent a semester exchange at University College Dublin. I completed a MSc in Economics at Barcelona GSE. My economic master thesis is Evolution of Cooperation in Multi-level Public Goods Game with Mobility, supervised by Professor Larbi Alaoui.
